Privacy Policy – Your Data, Clearly Protected

At https://www.immaculatedetailing.co.uk/, we place the highest priority on the privacy and security of our users. This Privacy Policy document outlines the types of information we collect, how it is used, protected, and your rights regarding your personal data. We are committed to full transparency and compliance with UK data protection laws, including the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018. Our goal is to ensure you have a safe, secure, and personalised experience while maintaining full control over your information.

What data we collect and how it powers your experience

We collect information that is essential for providing and improving our services. This includes:

  • Personal Identification Data: Such as your name, email address, date of birth, and postal address, which are required for account creation, verification, and communication.
  • Technical Data: Including your IP address, browser type, device information, and operating system. This helps us optimise website performance and security.
  • Usage Data: Details of your interactions with our site, such as pages visited, games played, and time spent. This information enables us to personalise content and improve user experience.
  • Financial Data: When you make transactions, we process payment details through secure, encrypted channels. We do not store full card numbers on our servers.

The essential role of cookies in customizing https://www.immaculatedetailing.co.uk/

Cookies are small text files placed on your device to enhance functionality and personalise your visit. We use different types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for core functions like secure login and session management. The website cannot operate properly without them.
  • Analytical/Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site, allowing us to improve navigation and content layout.
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember your preferences (like language or region) to provide a more tailored experience.
  • Targeting/Advertising Cookies: Used to deliver relevant promotions and content based on your interests, in compliance with advertising standards.

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time via your browser settings. For more details, please see our separate Cookie Policy.

How we responsibly use your data to improve our services

Your data is used strictly for legitimate business purposes and to enhance your experience with us. These purposes include:

  • To create and manage your account, verify your identity, and process transactions securely.
  • To provide customer support and respond to your inquiries effectively.
  • To personalise the website content, game recommendations, and promotional offers based on your activity.
  • To monitor and analyse site usage to improve performance, security, and develop new features.
  • To comply with legal obligations, such as age verification and anti-money laundering regulations under UKGC licence requirements.
  • To send important service updates, security alerts, and (with your consent) marketing communications about relevant promotions.

Strict protocols for sharing data with trusted partners

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information only with trusted third-party service providers under strict contractual agreements that mandate confidentiality and security. These partners include:

  • Payment Processors: Secure financial institutions necessary to handle deposits and withdrawals.
  • Game Providers: Licensed software partners to enable gameplay and ensure fairness.
  • Analytics Providers: Tools like Google Analytics (with anonymised data where possible) to help us understand site traffic.
  • Regulatory Bodies: When legally required, we may share information with the UK Gambling Commission or other authorities to fulfil our licensing obligations.

All data sharing is conducted in compliance with UK data protection law, and we ensure any international transfers provide an adequate level of data protection.

Your rights to access, modify, or delete your data anytime

Under UK data protection law, you have significant rights regarding your personal information. You can exercise these rights free of charge by contacting our Data Protection Officer at dpo@immaculatedetailing.co.uk. Your rights include:

  • Right of Access: To request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: To correct any inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure ('Right to be Forgotten'): To request deletion of your personal data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: To limit how we use your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: To receive your data in a structured, commonly used format.
  • Right to Object: To object to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing.
  • Rights related to automated decision-making: To request human intervention in automated processes.

We will respond to any such request within one month, as required by law.

Keeping you informed – timely updates on privacy policies

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, services, or legal requirements. Any significant changes will be communicated to you via email or through a prominent notice on our website prior to the change becoming effective. We encourage you to review this page regularly to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
